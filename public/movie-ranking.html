The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Greatest trilogy ever made. I could go on but won't. Content speaks for itself.

What Dreams May Come

Just a beautiful story starring Robin Williams about love, loss and grief. The cinematography is beautiful too. Quite unique.

Fist of Fury

First movie I recall seeing in my youth at the Drive-In. Was hooked on Bruce Lee and martial arts ever since.
